Privacy Policy for QuranFlow

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

QuranFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

QuranFlow does not require account registration and does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Camera Permissions and Usage

The app requests access to your device's Camera (android.permission.CAMERA) for the sole purpose of allowing users to capture images to use as custom background themes within the app.

  • Images are processed locally on your device
  • No images are uploaded to any server
  • No images are shared with any third parties

All captured content remains on your device.

3. Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect limited non-personal data such as device type, operating system version, and crash logs. This information is used only to improve app performance and fix issues.

4. Third-Party Services

QuranFlow does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or stored, your information remains on your device.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date.
