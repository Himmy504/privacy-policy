Privacy Policy for QuranFlow

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Welcome to QuranFlow. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is handled when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

QuranFlow is designed to respect your privacy. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number on our servers.

2. Camera Permissions and Usage

Our App requests access to your device's Camera (android.permission.CAMERA). This permission is solely required for the following purpose:

  • Custom Background Themes: The camera allows you to capture images to use as a personalized custom background theme within the app.

Zero Cloud Storage: Images captured using your camera are processed entirely locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your photos or camera stream with any external servers or third parties. Your images remain strictly on your device.

3. Other Data Collected Automatically

Like most mobile applications, we may automatically collect non-personal analytics or crash reports (e.g., device model, operating system version, and generalized non-personal usage data). This data helps us fix bugs and improve the overall performance and experience of the App.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party tracking, advertising, or data collection services that compromise your privacy.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
